The Online Screen Recorder

- Instructions:

  • Click on the "Start" Button to turn on the Screen Recorder.
  • Click on the "Stop" Button to turn off the Screen Recorder.
  • After Stopping the Screen Recorder Enter the "Filename".
  • Your File will get downloaded Automatically.

  • For the best user experience use Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge
